You will need to disable the two-factor authentication when this message appears. You will need to disable it in settings/privacy and security/two-factor authentication. You can only do this on your desktop computer. More info can be found here: https://help.instagram.com/566810106808145

2. Why am I getting an error when I try to unfollow someone?
If you try to follow, unfollow, like, or comment too fast, Instagram can trigger your account as a spam account. Try doing these actions (follow, unfollow, like, or comment )at a steady pace throughout the day. The newer your account, the higher chance your account will be triggered as a spam account when trying to complete these actions (follow, unfollow, like, or comment) too fast. When your account is labeled as a spam, you will have a set limit to follow, unfollow, like or comment per day. Your account can also be triggered as spam if you are using a proxy that is labeled as spam by Instagram. If these problems occur, don’t follow, unfollow, like, or comment for 12-24 hours and this problem will go away.
